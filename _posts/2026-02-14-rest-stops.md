A ride is only as good as its stops. This note is **planned** — a seed I keep adding to as
I find places worth the detour.

<!--more-->

## What makes a good stop

- **Shade and a wall to lean the bike against.** Non-negotiable in July.
- **Water you can refill, not just buy.**
- **A view that earns the parking.**

## The list so far

- *Rock Store, Mulholland* — the classic. Loud on weekends, quiet Tuesdays.
- *Ragged Point, Big Sur* — the halfway reset on [PCH](/notes/routes/pch/).
- *Newcomb's Ranch* — when it's open, the heart of Angeles Crest.

More to come. If a stop kept your ride from turning into a chore, it belongs here.
